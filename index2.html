Coalition to End Sexual Exploitation

The Coalition to End Sexual Exploitation is a conference I attended in both 2015 and 2016. People come from all over the country to share their knowledge about the harms of pornography, prostitution, sex trafficking, and other aspects of the sex industry.

Below are some helpful resources I obtained both at the conference and throughout my own research:

  • American Girls: Social Media and the Secret Lives of Teenagers by Nancy Jo Sales
  • Speak by Laurie Halse Anderson
  • In Our Backyard: Human Trafficking in America and What We Can Do to Stop It by Nita Belles
  • Paid For: My Journey Through Prostitution by Rachel Moran
  • Dirty Girls Come Clean by Crystal Renaud
  • Pornland: How Porn Has Hijacked Our Sexuality by Gail Dines

I also created a Twine that connects various aspects of the sex industry, such as pornography, prostitution, human trafficking, etc.
